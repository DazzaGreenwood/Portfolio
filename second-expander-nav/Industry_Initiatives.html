[The CU Ledger Summit](http://www.culedger.com/summit)

2016/6/5  posted in Industry Initiatives 

  • Join us for the CU Ledger Summit
  • June 29, 2016 - Minneapolis Mn

 Immediately following the AXFI Conference
Distributive Ledger Technology is one of the hottest topics in the financial industry, with Banks and Fintech putting millions into research and development. Immediately following the AXFI Conference, BIG, MWCUA and Evernym will hold a special meeting to discuss the latest news in Distributed Ledger Technology and Identity. Identity experts Drummond Reed and Phil Windley will deliver a presentation on Soveriegn Identity and what it means to have your very own Digital Identity . Come find out how the latest technology will solve the age old problem of "too many usernames and passwords" and how the Credit Union Industry will be a leader in this space.
