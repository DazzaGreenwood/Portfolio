    The Internet Council of NACHA, Certificate Authority Rating and Trust Task Force Guidelines, Herndon, VA [www.nacha.org]
    Chairman, Drafting Team Member, 1997 - 2000
    The report of this Task Force sets forth cutting edge thinking among public and private sector leading users and vendors of commercial public key cryptosystems.  This document details contractual and governance issues as well as particular technical design and implementation models for business systems using public key certificates to authenticate users over the Internet.  The Guidelines also offer specific guidance for organizations attempting to draft "certificate policies" according to the Internet Engineering Task Force Public Key Infrastructure (PKIX) Work Group Framework.  The report is available at [http://internetcouncil.nacha.org/carat.htm].
